BEFORE THE FALL: Los Santos was a city of dreams. Neon lights painted the nights in false promises, and people lived their lives believing tomorrow would always come. They called it paradise. They called it... Euphoria.

THE SCIENTIST: Dr. Irwin Grass worked in the shadows beneath the city. His laboratory, codenamed CROWN-13, promised humanity's next evolution. "We can transcend death itself," he whispered to his superiors. The government funded his obsession. They wanted soldiers that couldn't die.

THE FIRST BREACH: July 27th, 2025. Subject 001 broke containment. The screams echoed through the facility's corridors as Dr. Grass watched his life's work tear through security teams. Within hours, the infection spread through the underground tunnels like spilled blood seeking the light.

THE RED ZONE: The city fell in three days. Military cordons established the Red Zone - a quarantine that became a prison. Survivors speak of things worse than the infected. Shadows that move without bodies. Voices that call from empty buildings. Some say Dr. Grass is still down there, in his laboratory, continuing his work.

CURRENT STATUS: The gate he opened can't be closed. What began as a virus has become something... else. The infected evolve. Some remember who they were. Others hunt with purpose. And in the deepest parts of the city, something stirs that was never human at all.
